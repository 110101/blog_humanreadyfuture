What is the coolest area in a city by facts not by stories or urban legends? And which area could be the next hippster hot spot? This question came into my mind when I was walking through Munich and I was seeing lots of new organic stores, coffee shops and trendy restaurants popping up. Is there a way to determine the coolness of a neighborhood by data?
