Thoughts

#human-ready future: As an engineer and tech enthusiast I am convinced of the opportunities created by new technology. But I am also see the flip side with the risks that arise, if technology is not used with good intentions. If you look at the history where tech was not always used in a good way and today it's more relevant than ever. Most recent examples are the political power of social networks or biased machine learning algorithms.
I think and write about the interaction between technology, society and a code of ethics for the development of new technologies.
